# BOLDR CS SOP — Parsed Routing Rules

> Parsed from 05_cs_sop.docx | Author: Steve Ng, Founder and CEO — Digital Futures Consultancy LLP

## Tone Guidelines

- **Friendly but not overly casual** — we are a premium brand
- **Direct** — answer the question clearly, don't pad with filler
- **Helpful** — if we can't help directly, point them somewhere useful
- **Never promise what you're not sure about** — check first

### Good Opening
"Hi [Name], thanks for reaching out! Happy to help with that."

### Avoid
- Starting every reply with "Great question!"
- Using overly formal language ("Dear Sir/Madam")
- Making promises about timelines you cannot guarantee

## Escalation Triggers

Escalate to team lead whenever:
- A customer is angry or threatening a chargeback
- A warranty claim involves significant damage or potential product defect
- A refund has been outstanding for more than 10 business days
- A corporate or bulk order enquiry comes in (more than 5 units)
- You are not sure of the answer and cannot find it in the reference documents
- A media or press enquiry comes in — forward to marketing@boldr.co

## Engraving Rules

- Standard text engraving: up to 40 characters including spaces
- Pricing: SGD 25 for up to 20 chars, SGD 35 for 21-40 chars
- Within 1 hour of order: amend for free
- 1-24 hours after order: SGD 15 amendment fee
- After production started: not possible
- Logo/custom artwork: SGD 60, requires vector file (.ai or .svg)
- ⚠ Engraved items cannot be returned unless manufacturing defect

## Returns & Warranty Rules

- Returns accepted within 14 days for unworn, unaltered items
- Engraved items: non-returnable unless manufacturing defect
- Warranty: 2 years on movement. Does NOT cover physical damage, water damage from misuse, or strap wear
- Warranty claim: ask for photos/video of defect, flag to team
