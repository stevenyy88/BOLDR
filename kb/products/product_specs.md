## Quick Answers

| Customer asks... | Quick answer |
|---|---|
| Is the strap BPA-free? | Yes — all FKM rubber and nylon straps are BPA-free. Leather is BPA-free but not hypoallergenic. |
| Is it hypoallergenic? | Yes for titanium case and FKM/nylon straps. Leather straps: no. |
| What lug width? | 20mm — fits all standard 20mm third-party straps. |
| Can I swim with it? | Expedition: yes (100m). Journey: splash-resistant only, not for swimming. |
| Is the lume safe? | Yes — Super-LumiNova, non-radioactive, ISO 3157 compliant. |
| What is the warranty? | 2 years on movement. Does not cover physical damage or strap wear. |
| Is the Ember still available? | No — sold out. Direct to waitlist at boldr.co/waitlist. |
| Expedition vs Journey — which to choose? | Expedition: larger (40mm), more rugged, 100m WR, Grade 5 titanium. Journey: smaller (38mm), lighter, everyday wear, 50m WR, Grade 2 titanium. |
